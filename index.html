What We Do

Community Programs Built for Impact

🌿

Our three core programs address the most pressing environmental concerns in our barangay — working together to create lasting, positive change.

Active

Tree Planting

Every month, our volunteers gather to plant native tree species in vacant lots, school grounds, and roadsides — restoring green cover one sapling at a time.

Learn more
Ongoing

Waste Segregation

We conduct household workshops and distribute color-coded bins to promote proper waste sorting — reducing landfill burden and increasing recyclable recovery rates.

Learn more
Monthly

Clean Drive

Our barangay-wide clean-up events bring together residents to sweep streets, clear esteros, and remove illegal dumpsites — building community pride along the way.

Get Involved

Become a GreenBarangay Volunteer

🌿

What You'll Do

Volunteers are the heart of GreenBarangay. Here's how you can contribute:

  • Join monthly tree planting events in your purok
  • Help lead waste segregation workshops for households
  • Participate in community clean drives every first Saturday
  • Assist in distributing eco-bags and segregation kits
  • Share updates on social media to spread awareness

Ready to Make a Difference?

No experience needed — just the heart to serve. Sign up as a volunteer and we'll match you with the program that fits your schedule and interests best.

All volunteers receive a GreenBarangay shirt, eco-kit, and certificate of participation.
